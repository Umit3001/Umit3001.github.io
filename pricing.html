What brings the company

Innovation is by definition something which delivers an advantage; this may involve new sports, accessibility, rules, training methods, movement techniques, nutrition, materials and information systems. Innovations of the same kind as the clap skate (‘revolutionary innovations’) are the exception rather than the rule. Often thought to technical innovations (‘tech’), but innovations can equally well be ‘no-tech’; think of the revolutionary Fosbury Flop in the high jump, whereby the jumper clears the bar headfirst and backwards, and a sport like beach volleyball, evolved from a leisure activity at the shore.
